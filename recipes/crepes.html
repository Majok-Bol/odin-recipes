Basic Crepes

Description

Whip up a batch of light and delicate egg-white crepes with our simple recipe. These versatile treats can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or even as a delectable dessert. Using only egg whites, you'll create a healthier twist on the classic crepe while still achieving a delightful texture and flavor. With just a few staple ingredients, you'll be on your way to creating a stack of these paper-thin delights. Fill them with your favorite sweet or savory fillings, from fresh fruits and whipped cream to savory meats and cheeses. Our step-by-step instructions will guide you through the process, ensuring your egg-white crepes turn out perfectly every time. Impress your family and friends with this elegant and delicious culinary creation!

Ingredients

  • Egg Whites: Use about 4-6 large egg whites, depending on the desired serving size.
  • All-Purpose Flour: Measure out 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour. It helps provide structure to the crepes.
  • Milk: Use 1/2 cup of milk. You can opt for low-fat or skim milk if desired.
  • Salt: Add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavor of the crepes.
  • Cooking Spray or Butter: Use cooking spray or a small amount of butter to grease the pan for cooking the crepes.
