Baby Spinach Omelet

Description

Discover the art of creating mouthwatering omelets with our collection of egg-cellent recipes. Start your culinary journey today and impress your family and friends with your egg-cellent omelet-making skills!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves (washed and dried)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings/fillings (such as cheese, diced tomatoes, mushrooms, onions, etc.)
